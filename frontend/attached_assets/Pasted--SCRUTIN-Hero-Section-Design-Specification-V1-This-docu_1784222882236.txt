# SCRUTIN — Hero Section Design Specification (V1)

> This document defines only the Hero section of the Scrutin landing page.
> It does NOT describe the entire homepage.
> The objective is to recreate a premium editorial AI landing page similar in craftsmanship to the highest-end AI startups, while establishing Scrutin's own visual identity.

---

# 1. Design Philosophy

The Hero must communicate **trust before intelligence**.

Users visiting Scrutin are not looking for another AI chatbot.

They are trying to determine whether something is true.

Therefore the hero should feel like

• calm

• trustworthy

• premium

• editorial

• modern

• sophisticated

NOT

• cyberpunk

• hacker

• neon

• futuristic dashboard

• glassmorphism overload

The emotional response should be

"I trust this."

rather than

"This looks like AI."

---

# 2. Inspiration

Visual language should take inspiration from

- Incredible AI
- Linear
- Raycast
- Vercel
- Stripe Sessions
- Notion AI

while creating an original composition.

Never copy layouts directly.

# 17. What Must Never Be Used

No glowing neon.

No floating AI brains.

No random particles.

No blockchain graphics.

No circuit board backgrounds.

No holograms.

No sci-fi UI.

No unnecessary glass cards.

No excessive gradients.

No AI-generated clutter.

Minimalism wins.

---

# Final Goal

When someone lands on Scrutin for the first time, they should immediately feel:

• This is serious.

• This is trustworthy.

• This feels like Apple × Stripe × Perplexity.

Only after that should they discover the underlying multi-agent intelligence.
